Crystal Collector

You will be provided with a random number between 19 to 120. There are 4 crystals below (each with different values) that you must click and add up to the random number provided. If you achieve that number, you win, otherwise you lose. Everytime the game starts, the value of the crystals and the random number changes. Good luck!
